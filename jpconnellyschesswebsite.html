Bio and Background

Hello, my name is JP Connelly and welcome to my personal chess webpage! I am 18 years old and currently a Freshman at Northern Kentucky University. I am also working a part time job right now at Larosas, a local family pizzeria and Italian restaurant. I grew up in a small suburban city called Taylor Mill, just about 10 minutes away from Cincinnati, Ohio. Growing up in this small town I discovered many fun activities and hobbies I enjoyed doing and I still do to this day. My main hobby that I have been practicing a lot lately and I am very passionate about is Chess.

I learned chess at around the age of seven, and I instantly gained a love for it. I am a very visual learner so I picked up chess fairly quickly I'd say. My dad was the person that taught it to me and I am forever greatful that he did because I enjoy chess a lot for it is a lovely game. It was always my main goal to be able to beat him one day and I was never able to until just recently and I can't express how accomplished I felt when I did beat him. If you feel the same way about chess and want to beat someone you have never been able to beat I have created a brief list of a couple chess traps I have learned over the years that you may try if you ever want to pull a fast one on somebody. So go ahead, click one and learn in my opinion some of the most satisfying traps in chess. Please Enjoy!
